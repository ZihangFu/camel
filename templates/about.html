About Us

Camel Global is dedicated to providing an extensive range of high-quality packaging and hygiene solutions, with a focus on sustainability and exceptional service. Based in City, Auckland, we meet the diverse needs of our clients nationwide.

Under the visionary leadership of Jenny, Camel Global was established in 2024, we firmly believe we can evolve from humble beginnings into a trusted industry leader. Starting with a single living paper product, we will grow into a company renowned for innovative solutions and a customer-centric approach.

At Camel Global, we prioritize quality in every aspect of our business - from the products we offer to the services we deliver, and the people we employ. Unlike corporate giants, we value and nurture relationships with our clients and suppliers, upholding traditional values of honesty, integrity, and reliability.

Our commitment to excellence and our refreshing approach to business have earned us a reputation for being a reliable partner in the packaging and hygiene industry. We look forward to continuing our journey of growth and success, always putting our customers first.
